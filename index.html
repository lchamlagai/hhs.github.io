Health and Education for All

(HAEFA, pronounced hi-fa) is a US-based, 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.

SINCE 2012, OUR MEDICAL TEAMS HAVE PROVIDED FREE HEALTH SCREENING AND MEDICAL TREATMENT TO OVER 6.2 MILLION UNDERSERVED PEOPLE LIVING IN REMOTE AND COASTAL REGIONS OF BANGLADESH AND 1.1 MILLION ROHINGYA REFUGEES FROM MYANMAR.
